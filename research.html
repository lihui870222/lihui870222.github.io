Ongoing works

Disruption of GMCs with radiation-live dust coupling

Using a "live-dust" implementation, I investigate how dust particles decouple from gas cells in turbulent clouds. After a few dynamical times, the cloud shows a large range of dust-to-gas ratio in different regions, which strongly affects the effects of radiative pressure from massive stars onto the dust.

Galaxy mergers revist: a SMUGGLE view

Using SMUGGLE in Arepo, I study how galaxy mergers affect the GMC populations and the formation of most massive GMCs and bound star cluster.
