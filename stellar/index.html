Hui Li

I am currently a Postdoctoral Associate in the Computational Structure and Galaxy Formation Group at the MIT Kavli Institute for Astrophysics and Space Research. I received my Ph.D degree in the Astronomy Department, University of Michigan. Before coming to the US, I got my Master and Bachelor's degree in Nanjing University, China.
